  • 国内较快的仓库：

    maven {url’http://maven.aliyun.com/nexus/content/groups/public/'}

  • RecyclerView添加依赖

    compile ‘com.android.support:recyclerview-v7:26+’

    自动添加依赖：insert imports on paste: None
    自动删除无用依赖：Optimize imports on the fly
